NIKECORE

This project was developed using Kotlin

APP


An application connecting consumers to extended digital touch points based on their physical activity and shopping behaviour. NikeCore helps you maintian healthy wellness parctices by incentivising your daily physical activities that turn into benefits you can use online or on physical nike stores.

The Challenge




How it started ?

After studying and working on several projects.This self initiated project was started to explore and experiment with the internal and external phone sensors along with the ArCore. This led to an idea of developing an application that helps you maintain healthy wellness practices by incentivising your daily physical activities that turn into benefits you can use online touch points or on physical touch points.




The idea

An application connecting consumers to extended digital touch points based on their physical activity and shopping behaviour.




Outcome & impact




Motivating users towards a healthier future

- Users are more motivated to keep their physical   activity constant and help promote   the nike brand. - The global market will be more aware of the   nike brand and their idea of promoting   healthy living. - The nike store sales will increase and their   products will have more recognition.

Opportunity & solutions



Features and technical requirements
- Use maps to explore areas with more tickets. - Use AR Camera to collect tickets and   earn rewards. - Enable location tracking and bluetooth to keep   yourself updated about your run. - Claim the earned rewards by NFC payment   method at any Nike Store. - Developed the app using several technologies   such as Jetpack components(Navigation,   Room), Coroutines, Google Maps and ARCore   to name a few.
